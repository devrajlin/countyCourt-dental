About Us

Welcome To County Court Dentistry

County Court Dentistry is a full-service dental clinic located at 201 County Court Boulevard in Brampton. For more than three decades, the clinic has proudly served the local community. Our team is committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism. We combine experience and clinical expertise to deliver dependable dental care. Patient comfort remains a core focus in every treatment we provide. We strive to create a welcoming and stress-free environment for every visit. We take the time to develop personalised treatment plans for each case. Our goal is to ensure every patient feels confident they are in trusted hands. We look forward to supporting your long-term oral health and confident smile.

How long do dental implants typically last?

expand_more

With advanced placement techniques and meticulous patient care, dental implants are engineered for longevity. While the crown may require replacement after 10-15 years due to normal wear, the titanium post integrated into the jawbone is designed to be a permanent structural solution.

Regular diagnostic checks and hygiene sanctuary visits ensure the integrity of the integrated implant architecture.

02

Is the implant procedure painful?

expand_more

Patient comfort is our primary directive. Most patients report minimal discomfort, often comparable to a routine extraction. We utilize advanced local anesthesia and offer sedation options for a completely relaxed experience.

Post-operative recovery is usually swift, managed with standard over-the-counter protocols.

03

What is Digital Smile Design?

expand_more

Digital Smile Design (DSD) is a technical planning tool that allows us to architect your new smile digitally before we begin. We use 3D scans and facial analysis to ensure the final result is perfectly harmonious with your features.

This blueprint allows for unparalleled precision and lets you preview your transformation beforehand.

04

How do I maintain my restoration?

expand_more

Maintenance follows the same principles as natural dentition: meticulous brushing, flossing, and professional cleanings. However, we may recommend specific technical tools like interdental brushes or water flossers.

Biannual system audits at our sanctuary ensure the long-term stability of your restorative components.

05

Do you offer emergency technical support?

expand_more

Yes, we prioritize structural failures or acute pain with same-day emergency triage. Our clinical team remains on standby to address urgent requirements and restore system integrity immediately.

Please contact our direct link for priority scheduling during such events.
